The Problem

Existing monitor-arm tablet mounts are designed for viewing, not input. The moment a stylus touches the screen, the arm wobbles. Structural flex and joint play make precise pen work impossible, turning a productivity tool into a source of frustration.

Standalone tablet stands solve stability but sacrifice integration. They consume valuable desk space, require separate cable management, and cannot match the ergonomic adjustability of a monitor arm.

Dedicated pen displays offer the best input experience, but at a steep cost: a separate device, separate power, separate mounting — duplicating hardware the user already owns in their tablet.

We solve all three through a single patented architecture.
