The Workflow Gap

Modern knowledge work spans multiple devices — displays, tablets, laptops, peripherals. Yet desk hardware has not evolved to integrate them into a single coherent workflow.

Existing solutions treat each device in isolation. The result: cluttered desks, fragmented workflows, and ergonomic compromises that hurt productivity.

We see the workstation as a single system. Our patented hardware architecture is built around that principle — engineered to eliminate the friction between devices, not just hold them.

One desk. One workflow. One integrated system.
